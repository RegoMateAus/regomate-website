How it works
Three steps to never forget again
01

Enter your details

Fill in your name, address, rego plate, and expiry dates. Takes under 2 minutes and you're set for the year.

02

We print & post your sticker

Your personalised sticker is automatically printed and posted within 3–5 business days of signing up. First year only.

03

SMS reminders keep you on track

We text you 6 weeks, 4 weeks, 2 weeks before expiry — then daily in the final week. Opt out instantly once you've renewed.
