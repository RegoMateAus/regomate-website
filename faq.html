🏷The service
What exactly is RegoMate? ›
RegoMate is a vehicle registration reminder service. When you sign up, we post you a personalised windscreen sticker printed with your rego plate, rego expiry date, and driver's licence expiry date. We also send you automatic SMS reminders before each expiry date so you never get caught out with an expired rego or licence.
Is RegoMate affiliated with the government or my state's transport authority? ›
No. RegoMate is a private service and is not affiliated with any Australian government, transport authority, or vehicle registration agency. We do not process or renew registrations on your behalf — we are purely a reminder and convenience tool. You are still responsible for renewing your rego and licence through your state's transport authority.
Does RegoMate automatically check my rego dates with the government? ›
No — RegoMate relies entirely on the expiry dates you provide when you sign up. We do not connect to any government database. This means it's important to enter your dates accurately, and to update them in your account if they change — for example if you renew early or get a new licence.
What states does RegoMate work in? ›
RegoMate works in all Australian states and territories — QLD, NSW, VIC, SA, WA, TAS, ACT, and NT. We post stickers to any Australian address and SMS reminders go to any Australian mobile number.
Can I add more than one vehicle? ›
Currently each RegoMate subscription covers one vehicle. If you have multiple vehicles, you can sign up for a separate subscription for each one. We're working on a multi-vehicle account feature — stay tuned.
📦Sticker & delivery
What comes in the post? ›
Your RegoMate pack contains two items:

1. Your personalised sticker — printed with your rego plate, rego expiry date, and driver's licence expiry date.
2. An alcohol wipe — to clean your windscreen surface before applying the sticker so it bonds properly and lasts.
Tip: Read our installation guide before you apply the sticker for the best result.
How long does delivery take? ›
Your sticker is printed and posted within 3–5 business days of your subscription being confirmed. Delivery is via Australia Post. Allow an additional 2–5 business days for delivery depending on your location.
Do I get a sticker every year? ›
No — your physical sticker is included in your first year only ($14.99). From year two onwards your subscription is $9.99/yr and covers SMS reminders only. Your sticker is designed to stay on your windscreen year after year.

You can order a replacement sticker at any time for $7.99 + $4.99 postage — for example if your sticker is damaged, lost, or your details have changed.
Where should I put the sticker on my car? ›
The sticker is designed to go on the inside of your windscreen only — not on the outside. Most people place it in the top corner on the driver's side, near where the registration label sits. It should be somewhere easy to see as a reminder but outside your direct line of sight while driving.
Make sure your windscreen is cool and at room temperature before applying — a hot windscreen prevents the adhesive bonding correctly. See our installation guide for full instructions.
My sticker details are wrong. What do I do? ›
Log in to your RegoMate account and update your rego plate, rego expiry, or licence expiry in the Vehicle details section. Your SMS reminders will automatically use the corrected dates. You can then order a replacement sticker with the correct details for $12.98 (sticker + postage).
My sticker arrived damaged or didn't arrive. What happens? ›
Contact us at support@regomate.com with your order number and a photo of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement at no charge. If your sticker hasn't arrived within 10 business days of dispatch, please get in touch and we'll look into it.
Can I order a replacement sticker if I get a new car or new licence? ›
Yes — you can order a replacement sticker at any time while your subscription is active. Go to the replacement sticker page, log in, and your current details will be pre-filled. Just update anything that's changed and complete the order. Cost is $7.99 + $4.99 postage and handling ($12.98 total).
📱SMS reminders
When will I receive SMS reminders? ›
We send SMS reminders at the following intervals before both your rego expiry date and your licence expiry date — independently:

● 6 weeks before — first heads-up, plenty of time to plan
● 4 weeks before — time to book it in
● 2 weeks before — getting close, action recommended
● Every day in the final week — daily until you've renewed
You get two sets of reminders — one for your rego expiry and one for your licence expiry. If both fall in the same month you'll receive reminders for both.
I've renewed my rego. How do I stop the reminders? ›
Simply reply STOP to any reminder SMS and your reminders will pause immediately. You can also log in to your account and pause them there.
Important: Pausing reminders only applies to the current renewal cycle. Your reminders will automatically resume 6 weeks before next year's expiry date — you don't need to do anything. If you want to permanently stop all reminders, you can cancel your subscription.
Why am I still getting reminders after I replied STOP? ›
STOP replies are processed automatically and should take effect immediately. If you are still receiving messages after replying STOP, contact us at support@regomate.com and we will resolve it straight away. Please note that if you have both a rego reminder and a licence reminder active, replying STOP to one will pause that series — you may still receive the other series until you reply STOP to that message as well, or manage both in your account.
I changed my mobile number. Will I still get reminders? ›
No — reminders go to the mobile number registered on your account. If you change your number, log in to your account and update it in the Personal details section as soon as possible to avoid missing reminders.
Will I get SMS about other RegoMate products? ›
Occasionally we may send you information about new RegoMate products or features that we think you'll find useful. You can opt out of these marketing messages at any time by replying STOP or managing your preferences in your account — this won't affect your renewal reminders.
I didn't receive a reminder I was expecting. What happened? ›
This can be caused by a few things — your mobile number may be incorrect in your account, your carrier may have filtered the message, or your phone may have blocked the number. Check your account details are correct, then contact us at support@regomate.com if the problem continues. Note that RegoMate is a convenience tool — you remain responsible for renewing your rego and licence on time regardless of whether reminders are received.
💰Account & billing

Year 1 — $14.99 — includes your personalised sticker, alcohol wipe posted to you, and all SMS reminders for 12 months.

Year 2+ — $9.99/yr — SMS reminders continue automatically. No sticker is included from year two (your windscreen sticker is designed to stay put). You can order a replacement sticker anytime for $12.98.

You can cancel at any time by logging into your account and selecting Cancel subscription, or by emailing us at support@regomate.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current subscription period — you won't be charged again. No partial refunds are provided for unused portions of the subscription period.
Can I get a refund? ›
Fees are non-refundable unless required by Australian Consumer Law. If you believe you are entitled to a refund, contact us at support@regomate.com and we will assess your request in accordance with our obligations under the Australian Consumer Law.
How do I update my details? ›
Log in to your account and you can update your personal details (name, address, email, mobile), vehicle details (plate, rego expiry, licence expiry), and SMS preferences at any time. Changes take effect immediately — updated expiry dates will be used for all future reminders.
Is my payment information secure? ›
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, a world-leading payment processor. We never store your full card details — only Stripe handles your payment information. All traffic on the RegoMate website is encrypted via HTTPS.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ›
On the login page, click Forgot your password? and enter your email address. We'll send you a reset link. If you don't receive it within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact us at support@regomate.com.
How do I delete my account? ›
You can request account deletion by emailing support@regomate.com. We will delete your personal information within a reasonable time, unless we are required to retain it by law. Deleting your account will also cancel your subscription.
🏷Installation
How do I apply the sticker? ›
See our full step-by-step installation guide. The short version: make sure your windscreen is cool, use the included alcohol wipe to clean the spot thoroughly, let it dry for 30 seconds, then apply the sticker from one edge across, pressing firmly.
Why won't my sticker stick? ›
The most common cause is a surface that wasn't cleaned or dried properly before applying. Make sure you:

● Use the alcohol wipe to clean the area firmly
● Wait until the surface is completely dry (about 30 seconds)
● Ensure the windscreen is cool — not hot from sun or a car heater
● Don't touch the adhesive side with your fingers
● Press firmly across the whole sticker for 10–15 seconds
I lost the alcohol wipe. Can I still apply the sticker? ›
Yes — any isopropyl alcohol wipe (available from chemists and supermarkets) works just as well. You can also use methylated spirits on a lint-free cloth. Avoid household glass cleaners as they can leave a residue that prevents the adhesive bonding properly.
How do I remove the sticker if I need to? ›
Gently warm the sticker with a hair dryer on a low setting for 20–30 seconds to soften the adhesive, then carefully peel from one corner. Any remaining adhesive residue can be removed with a small amount of isopropyl alcohol on a cloth. Take care not to scratch the glass.
